The Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation

Not many people know that in 1985, when Bill Gates left Microsoft, he and his wife Melinda created The Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation. It was a non-profit organization aiming to help children worldwide with improving health and learning. Since then, they have awarded more than $30 billion to various initiatives. What most people don’t know is why they chose to get involved with philanthropy in such a large way. Most billionaires would either give away their money quietly or would set up their own charitable foundations through family members or close friends. But not Bill and Melinda; instead, they decided to give it all away through an entirely separate entity from themselves – The Gates Foundation. This is where things get interesting…

By setting aside his ego, by building a team around him, and by admitting mistakes. What is his secret?

Bill Gates has a secret you need to learn. He, along with countless others, will tell you it is all about hard work and sacrifice.
